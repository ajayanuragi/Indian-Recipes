Red Lentil Curry

What Is Red Lentil Curry?

This red lentil curry is rich and hearty. It's great as a main meal rather than a side dish like the more traditional Indian dhal.

How to Make Red Lentil Curry?

  • Step 1

    Wash lentils in cold water until water runs clear.

  • Step 2

    Put lentils in a pot with enough water to cover; bring to a boil and reduce heat to medium-low.
    Cover and simmer, adding water as needed to keep lentils covered, until tender, 15 to 20 minutes. Drain.

  • Step 3

    Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat; cook and stir onions in hot oil until caramelized, about 20 minutes.

  • Step 4

    Mix together curry paste, curry powder, turmeric, cumin, chili powder, salt, sugar, garlic, and ginger in a large bowl; stir into onions.
    Increase heat to high and cook, stirring constantly, until fragrant, 1 to 2 minutes.

  • Step 5

    Stir in tomato puree and lentils; cook until warmed through.
